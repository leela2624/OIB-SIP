Developed an Android App

An Android application that provides real-time weather information to users based on their current location or a location of their choice. The app fetches data from a weather API and displays current weather conditions, temperature, humidity, wind speed, and forecasts. Users can also set up notifications for weather alerts and customize the app's theme.


Student Attendence Management System

A web-based application designed to streamline the process of recording and managing student attendance for educational institutions. The system allows teachers or administrators to log attendance for individual students, view attendance reports, and track student attendance trends over time. Features include user authentication, attendance tracking by date and class, generating attendance reports, and notifications for low attendance or absenteeism. The system helps educational institutions maintain accurate attendance records, identify patterns of attendance, and improve overall student engagement and academic performance.
